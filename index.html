About me

I'm a scientist in the field of regulatory genomics. I recently (Dec 2016) completed my PhD in the genetics of human gene expression and its relationship to obesity associated diseases. During this time, I used several statistical approaches to understand how gene expression is regulated at the genetic level and how genetic variants interact with the environment. In April 2017 I will start as Postdoctoral research with Cecelia Lindgren at the Big Data Institute, part of the Univeristy of Oxford. My focus will be using machine learning methods to understand regulatory genetics with a focus on adipose/adipocyte biology. In addition to this, I am recently a keen kaggler. Kaggle is a machine learning competition community and I am currently part of a competition that is focused on using image classification to help combat the illegal tuna fishing trade.
