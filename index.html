About me

Hi! I'm Craig Glastonbury, a scientist working in the field of regulatory genomics. I recently (Dec 2016) completed my PhD in the genetics of human gene expression and its relationship to obesity associated diseases. During this time, I used several statistical approaches to understand how gene expression is regulated at the genetic level and how genetic variants interact with the environment. In April 2017 I started as a Postdoctoral research scientist with Cecilia Lindgren at the Big Data Institute, part of the Univeristy of Oxford. My focus will be using machine learning methods to understand regulatory genetics with a focus on adipose/adipocyte biology. In addition to this, I am a keen kaggler. Kaggle is a machine learning competition community and I am currently a kaggle 'competition master', ranking in the top 0.86% of players globally (524th / 60,593)
